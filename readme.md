 ## :camera: How to run :

1. Just open the site
2. Fill  'Enter a Place location :' column. It's just a name that will be shown as popus over the marker in map. 

<p align="center">
    <img alt="Home screen" src="./screenshots/Screenshot 2.png" width="80%"/>
</p>




3. Fill  'Enter a latitude :' and 'Enter a longitude :' columns with the desired latitude and longitude of area that you wanna analyse. Just find the location on maps .Then from url ,you can easily obtain latitude and longitude just as shown below.
<p align="center">
    <img alt="Home screen" src="./screenshots/Screenshot 1.png" width="80%"/>
</p>


4. Fill 'Enter an radius (in meters) :' column which represents the radius of the region to be analyzed.
5. Fill 'Enter the amenities you are searhing :' column like : PG,Hostel,Appartment (all values must be in lower case).
6. Fill 'What are the facilities you are expecting :' column. List of the facilities that you would like to consider in our analysis

7. Then just click : 'show result button' and done.
